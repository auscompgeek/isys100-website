Notable people and organisations

There are many people (and groups of people) who have contributed in the field of computer and information security.

Name Picture Website Description
Aleksa Serai cyphar.com Has contributed patches to the Linux kernel and Docker (a wrapper around Linux container features) improving security. Also found security flaws in notable services such as Optus' voicemail (with Shubham Shah) and Medium.
Shubham Shah shubh.am Found and reported security flaws in voicemail services, including Optus (alongside Aleksa Serai) and Vodafone.
Ben Grubb bengrubb.com Deputy technology editor for the Sydney Morning Herald. Reported on Shubham and Aleksa's voicemail security flaws.
Gibson Security gibsonsec.org Discovered security flaws in the popular Snapchat app.
